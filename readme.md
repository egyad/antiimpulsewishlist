## What is Anti-Impulse Wishlist?

Anti-Impulse Wishlist is a mindful shopping companion that helps you pause
before you buy. Instead of purchasing on impulse, you add items to a cooling
list and wait a set number of days. When the timer expires you decide — keep
wanting it, or let it go. Over time the app shows you how much money you've
saved and how many impulse buys you avoided.

## Core features

- **Add items** — capture a product name, price, optional URL, and a trigger
  tag (e.g. "boredom", "FOMO") to understand your spending triggers.
- **Cooling countdown** — each item has a user-defined cooling period (1–30 days).
  A notification fires when an item is ready to review.
- **Decision moment** — dismiss the item (count it as a save) or keep it in
  your list.
- **Progress dashboard** — track total dismissed, total saved, money saved,
  current streak, and rank milestones.
- **Library** — browse your full history of kept and dismissed items, with
  swipe-to-dismiss support.
- **Zen mode** — a distraction-free view when you just need a moment.
- **Analytics report** — see spending trigger breakdowns and personal patterns
  over time.
- **Confetti milestones** — reduced-motion-aware celebrations at key savings
  thresholds.

## Privacy at a glance

- All wishlist data stays **on your device** — nothing is synced to a server.
- Optional, anonymous analytics help improve the app (PostHog).
- Optional crash reporting helps fix bugs (Sentry).
- No ads. No advertising identifiers. No data sold.
